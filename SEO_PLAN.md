# SEO Plan & Expansion Strategy: Alpha Clean Group

## 1. Executive Summary & GSC Analysis
**Status:** The site is ranking for high-intent keywords but often on page 2-5 (positions 20-80), indicating high relevance but low authority/specificity.
**Key Findings from Search Console:**
- **Top Query Categories:** Childcare, Medical, Warehouse, Strata, and Gym cleaning.
- **Top Locations:** Sydney (broad), Wetherill Park, Silverwater, Auburn, Parramatta.
- **Missed Opportunities:**
  - `medical cleaning sydney`: 489 Impressions, Position 49. (Needs dedicated strong page).
  - `warehouse cleaning sydney`: 313 Impressions, Position 74.
  - `childcare centre cleaning sydney`: 303 Impressions, Position 85.
  - `strata cleaning services sydney`: 268 Impressions, Position 89.
  - **Specific Suburbs:** Users are specifically searching for "Office cleaning Silverwater" and "Wetherill Park".

**Strategy:** Create highly specific **Service + Location** pages to capture this long-tail traffic. By targeting specific suburbs (e.g., "Medical Cleaning Parramatta"), we face less competition and gain higher relevance signals, which will also boost the main "Sydney" pages.

## 2. Design & Conversion Optimization (100% Impact)
*To maximize conversions and trust, the following design elements are mandatory for every new page.*

### A. Color Psychology & Branding
- **Primary Color:** Use the brand Green (`#108602`) heavily for primary buttons, icons, and accents to signify cleanliness, growth, and eco-friendliness.
- **Contrast:** Use high-contrast whitespace to make the content readable and professional.
- **Action Color:** Ensure "Get a Quote" buttons stand out against the white/gray backgrounds.

### B. Contact Number Visibility
- **Sticky Header:** The phone number **02 9140 6024** must be visible at all times in the header.
- **Hero Section:** Display the number prominently in the Hero section of every landing page.
- **Footer:** Repeat the number with a clickable `tel:` link in the footer.
- **Floating CTA:** Consider a mobile-only floating "Call Now" button for users on the go.

### C. Trust Signals
- Display "5-Star Google Reviews" and "Insured & Bonded" badges near the contact forms.
- Use real images of cleaners in uniform where possible (or high-quality stock from the `generate_image` tool if needed).
